Press space

The Public Interest Entrepreneurs program aims to bring together people from outside the administration with advanced digital skills - data science, development and design - and public servants committed to innovation. Public Interest Entrepreneurs are divided into multidisciplinary twosomes or threesomes. With their mentors, they have 10 months to meet the challenge of improving public services using digital technology and data.
